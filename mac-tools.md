### Screenshots
#### (Built-in commands)
* __Command + Shift + 3__: Take a screenshot
* __Command + Shift + 4__: Take a snapshot of a designated region on screen
* __Command + Shift + 4 + spacebar__: Take a video of a designated region on screen
